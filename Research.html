Welcome to Our Group!

Our research group aims at achieving smart zero-energy and zero-carbon district energy systems for carbon neutrality and climate change mitigation, via cleaner power production, energy-efficient system design and operation, innovation in smart energy integration, and multi-objective optimization on nonlinear dynamic behaviours with artificial intelligence. Cross-disciplinary areas and multidisciplinary research with cutting-edge technologies are required, including materials engineering, chemical engineering, thermodynamics, computational fluid dynamics, renewable energy engineering, energy economics, and artificial intelligence. His current research interests include electrochemical batteries, hydrogen and pumped hydro storages in zero-energy buildings, life-cycle carbon-neutral buildings, peer-to-peer energy trading and inter-city energy migration energy network with hydrogen economy. We are continuously contributing to:

  • Low-energy and zero-energy buildings
    Latent heat storage with phase change materials
    Thermodynamics with computational fluid dynamics
    Building energy simulation and analytics

  • Building integrated renewable energy systems
    Solar heating and cooling
    BIPV and solar cell cooling
    Heat pumps
    Micro-wind turbine

  • Smart buildings with artificial intelligence
    Degradation models on battery cycling ageing and hydrogen fuel cells
    Energy storage and smart grid: battery, fuel cell-based hydrogen
    Resilient regional interactive energy sharing and inter-city energy migration network
    Dynamic performance prediction and optimisation

  • Lifecycle zero-carbon buildings
    Embodied carbon emission;
    Carbon offsetting technologies
