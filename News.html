Dr. Yuekuan ZHOU is appointed as Editorial Board Member of Journal of Thermal Science and Engineering (TSE)

TSE is an international open access journal that publishes high-quality research articles that span activities ranging from fundamental thermodynamic scientific research to the applied discussion of maximising thermodynamic efficiencies and minimising all heat losses.
